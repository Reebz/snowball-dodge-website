Snowball Dodge

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Snowball Dodge does not collect your data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. Nothing you do in the game is sent to us.

This policy explains how Snowball Dodge (the “app”) and its website handle your information. Snowball Dodge is made by Capita Technology (“we,” “us”). It is a simple, free arcade game.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. We have no servers that receive your data, no user accounts, and no way to identify you.

The app saves a few settings on your device — things like your best score, your chosen character, and whether sound is on. This information stays on your device, is only used to run the game, and is never sent to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Game Center leaderboard

If you choose to use the leaderboard, that feature is provided by Apple’s Game Center. When you submit a score, the app sends it to Game Center, which is run by Apple. Your Game Center nickname and account are managed by Apple, not by us. We do not receive your Apple ID, your real name, or any account details.

Your use of Game Center is covered by Apple’s Privacy Policy. You can turn Game Center off in your device’s Settings at any time.

Purchases

Snowball Dodge offers optional cosmetic characters you can buy. These purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store’s in-app purchase system. Apple processes the payment. We never see or store your payment card, billing address, or Apple ID. Apple handles that information under its own privacy policy.

No tracking, analytics, or ads

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party tracking of any kind. The app includes no advertising SDKs and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Children’s privacy

Snowball Dodge is family-friendly and safe for players of any age. Because we collect no personal information from anyone, we collect none from children either. We do not knowingly gather data from children, and there is nothing to gather.

This website

This website (snowballdodge.com) is a simple, static marketing page. We do not use cookies or analytics on it. It is hosted by Vercel, which, like any web host, may keep standard server logs (such as IP addresses and request times) for security and reliability. We do not use those logs to identify you, and we do not have access to them for marketing purposes. See Vercel’s Privacy Policy for details.

Third-party services

The only third party involved in the app is Apple, which provides the App Store, in-app purchases, and Game Center. Apple’s handling of your information is described in Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and change the “Last updated” date above. Because the app collects no data, any changes are likely to be small clarifications.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at contact@snowballdodge.com.
